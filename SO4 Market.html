Engine

Built for traders who care where their fills come from.

so4 runs a unified liquidity layer — one pool backs every market, with deterministic settlement on every fill. No hidden routes, no opaque MM rebates.

On-chain orderbook

Every quote, fill, and liquidation lands in a verifiable block. No off-chain matching engine, no batch tricks — just a transparent ledger you can audit.

Median latency38ms

Up to 50× leverage

Cross- and isolated-margin modes, per-market caps, and a partial-liquidation engine that protects healthy positions during volatility spikes.

Max leverage50×

Self-custodied

Your keys, your collateral. No deposits to a custodian, no withdrawal queues. Pull your margin on the same block you close a position.

Withdraw time< 1 block

Sub-second matching

A purpose-built sequencer commits orders in 200ms blocks. Cancels and replaces are first-class — no priority gas auctions to game.

Block time200ms
